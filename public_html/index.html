Text Randomizer

Browser-side randomizer with unique output, synonyms, permutations, unlimited nesting, escaped symbols, separator shuffle, %rand%, and Text/JSON/CSV output modes.

Syntax

  • {text 1|text 2|text 3} - alternatives (synonyms)
  • {|text} - optional fragment
  • [text 1|text 2|text 3] - permutation (shuffle)
  • [+delimiter+text 1|text 2|text 3] - shuffle with separator
  • \{ \} \| \[ \] \+ \\ - escaping reserved characters
  • %rand% - random digit from 0 to 9
  • | at top level works as paragraph shuffle
  • All operations support nesting
